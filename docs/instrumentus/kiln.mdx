The Kiln is a new Furnace Variant added by Instrumentus that excels at smelting at lot of the building-block related recipes that the Blast Furnace and the Smoker cannot smelt.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/11f4dbc0-3cfb-44c2-858c-87089830fc30)

### Crafting

The Kiln can be crafted by following the recipe below.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cfe9dc78-d513-4451-b6ed-3dc44c2dc54c)

### Example Uses

The Kiln smelts things like Sand, Clay, Netherrack, and other building-block related smelting recipes at a faster rate, with adjusted fuel efficiency similar to the Smoker and the Blast Furnace. Some example recipes are below, though you can see a full list of the recipes that the Kiln can perform using JEI.
